Caleb is right. We are friendly at Waldorf University
– and we’d love for you to visit.
{{#markdown}} Waldorf University is a private, liberal arts school located in the heart of beautiful Forest City, Iowa. We are located centrally among seven of the Midwest’s most populated metropolitan regions – so you can experience the best of both worlds – the small-town charm of Forest City and the big city appeal of Des Moines, Chicago, Minneapolis and more – all within driving distance. When you come to campus, you’ll receive a personal tour from one of our student ambassadors, meet with our admissions team and have the opportunity to speak to a professor in your field of study. If you’re interested in fine arts or athletics, you’ll have the opportunity to visit with a director or coach as well. Just for visiting us, you’ll receive: + $500 travel grant + 20% discount at the Waldorf Bookstore + Admittance to an athletic or fine art event + Complimentary lunch We have an open house on Friday, April 21 that you won’t want to miss. For more information on this event, please visit [http://www.waldorf.edu/admissions/visit-waldorf](http://www.waldorf.edu/admissions/visit-waldorf?utm_source=NRCCUA&utm_medium=Email&utm_content=VisitCampus&utm_campaign=General) So, come see how friendly we are in Forest City, Iowa – and take a walk in a Warrior’s shoes. **#WeAreWarriors** {{/markdown}}

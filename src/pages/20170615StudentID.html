{{#markdown}} # Celebrate! You can now access your own Waldorf Student ID Card. Many businesses offer discounts or special offers to students. Download your Student ID card today and start saving! ### How to Access Your Card **Step 1:** Head to the [Student ID Card Page in Your Portal](https://mywaldorf.waldorf.edu/student/resources/StudentIDCard/).
**Step 2:** Click on the Student ID Link and another window will pop up.
**Step 3:** Make sure the information is correct on the card with your name and student number.
**Step 4:** Print off your new card and cut it out.
**Step 5:** Stick the card in your wallet and show off your Waldorf student card wherever you go! {{/markdown}}
{{#markdown}} Have questions? Email [students@waldorf.edu](mailto:students@waldorf.edu?subject="Help with Student ID Card") for help accessing your student ID Card. {{/markdown}}

--- subject: Online Fire Science Degrees ---
{{#markdown}} Interested in earning your fire science degree? Waldorf University offers Fire and Emergency Services Higher Education (FESHE)-recognized online fire science degrees that empower you to earn the promotion you deserve while keeping your current job a priority. Taught by some of the industry’s most experienced and respected firefighters, Waldorf’s fire science programs cover real-world topics including fire safety, investigation, leadership and administration. Students can choose from associate, bachelor’s or master’s degree programs including: * [A.A. Fire Science Administration](http://www.waldorf.edu/academics/degree-programs/bachelors/bas-fire-science-administration?utm_source=FireHouse&utm_medium=Email&utm_content=20170522_Between%2BResponses&utm_campaign=FireScience) * [B.S. Fire Science Administration (FESHE-Recognized)](http://www.waldorf.edu/academics/degree-programs/bachelors/bs-fire-science-administration?utm_source=FireHouse&utm_medium=Email&utm_content=20170522_Between%2BResponses&utm_campaign=FireScience) * [M.A. Fire/Rescue Executive Leadership](http://www.waldorf.edu/academics/degree-programs/masters/ms-organizational-leadership/fire-rescue-executive-leadership?utm_source=FireHouse&utm_medium=Email&utm_content=20170522_Between%2BResponses&utm_campaign=FireScience) {{/markdown}}
